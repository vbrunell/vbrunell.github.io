Welcome to my site

This site has (at least) three purposes:

  1. To display some of the projects I've worked on.

  2. To store links to content I find useful.

  3. To provide some useful tools and information to students in COP3014 at FSU.

About Me

I'm a graduate TA in the computer science department at Florida State University. Before entering the graduate program, I lived in Japan for five years and worked for the Ministry of Education in Tokushima prefecture. I speak and read Japanese, and would love to hear from you if you have some good Japanese sci-fi book recommendations (My personal favorite is M-9, because what's not to like about a meteorological agency that predicts giant monster attacks?). I'm interested in machine learning, computer architecture, and algorithm design and analysis. My favorite programming language is C++, but I've dabbled in Java, Python, Ruby, HTML5, and C as well.
